</b>          They turn as DORIAN clamors over the side of the boat. He's got a 
          gun pointed right at them.
<b>

          holes and holds it overboard.
<b>           
</b><b>          STANLEY
</b>          Hold it right there or you'll be
          looking for this on the bottom
          of the harbor.
<b>           
</b>          Dorian stops in his tracks.
<b>           
</b><b>          DORIAN
</b>          Drop it and I'll kill you all.
<b>           
</b><b>          STANLEY
</b>          You can have it. But she gets
          to go.
<b>

</b>          Fine.
<b>           
</b><b>          TINA
</b>          Go where?
<b>           
</b><b>          STANLEY
</b>          Swimming. We're still close to
          shore.

<b>

<b>           
</b><b>          STANLEY
</b>          Go ahead. Hurry...

</b>          Okay. Put it down. Right over
          there.

</b>          As Dorian turns to see where it lands, Stanley jumps him. Dorian 
          FIRES but misses. Stanley jumps Dorian -- knocking his gun away.

</b>          Dorian falls into one of the WALL TORCHES -- toppling it.

</b>          The TWO MEN slug it out as a FIRE STARTS. It burns closer and 
          closer to the dynamite -- the mask in the middle of the pile.

<b>           
</b><b>          TINA
</b>          (sees fire)
          Stanley! The dynamite!
<b>

<b>           
</b>          Beat. Stanley surfaces gasping for breath. Tina pulls him over to

</b>          standing on the remains of the boat. Like Wile E. Coyote, he's
